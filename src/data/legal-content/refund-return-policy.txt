Refund & Return Policy

Requests for returns or refunds must be submitted within 7 to 10 days of purchasing a product or service, or within 7 to 10 days from the date on which the service is delivered.

If you are not satisfied with your purchased subscription package, please submit a return request through the MacroHealthPlus website.

For selected products, a refund may be accepted if you change your mind. Please include your subscription ID and email your request to info@macrohealthplus.org.

Issuance of Refunds

The refund process begins after MacroHealthPlus has approved and processed your request according to the refund type.

The processing time depends on the type of refund and the payment method used.

The refund amount covers the item price shown at the time of purchase.
