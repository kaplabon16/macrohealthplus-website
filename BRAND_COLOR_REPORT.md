# Brand Color Report

## Detected Brand Colors
- Live site brand/action green observed in bundle/legal link styles: #69B128.
- Existing product imagery also uses healthcare green/teal accents.

## Final Dark-Mode Palette
- Pitch dark base: #050B14 / black sections.
- Soft dark surface: #08111F.
- Glass cards: rgba(255,255,255,0.06-0.10).
- Primary accent: #69B128.
- Secondary accent: #0EA5A4.
- Text: #F8FAFC, #CBD5E1, #94A3B8.

## Usage
Accent colors are used for CTA highlights, active nav glow, icons, pricing emphasis, and link accents while preserving the pitch-dark design.

## Contrast Notes
White and slate text are kept on dark surfaces. Green accents are mostly used with supporting white text rather than as body text on dark backgrounds.
